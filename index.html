Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before DevConf 2026.

Where is DevConf 2026 held?

The conference takes place at the Boston Convention & Exhibition Center, easily reachable by public transit and just 15 minutes from Logan International Airport.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, you can transfer your ticket to a colleague at no extra cost.

Are the sessions recorded?

Yes, all main-stage talks are recorded. Pro and Team ticket holders get lifetime access, while Standard attendees can view recordings for 30 days.

Is there a virtual attendance option?

DevConf 2026 is an in-person only event this year, designed around hands-on workshops and hallway networking that don't translate well to a livestream.

What should I bring?

Bring a laptop for workshops, a valid photo ID for badge pickup, and a reusable water bottle — we're keeping this year's event as low-waste as possible.
